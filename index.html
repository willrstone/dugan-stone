About Us

Dugan/Stone Trailer Co. is a full-service trailer manufacturer located right outside of Groesbeck, TX on Hwy 164 headed towards Mart. In addition to new trailers, we offer maintenance, repairs, alterations, and a variety of trailer parts.

Trailers

  • - New livestock, hay, and utility trailers
  • - Repairs and refurbishing for all trailers
  • - Escape doors, dog noses, etc.
  • - Sandblasting and paint services
  • - Upgrades and alterations
  • - Trailer maintenance

Products

  • - Shelby rubber flooring
  • - Dexter axles
  • - Canvas tops
  • - Wheels and tires
  • - Lights and wiring
  • - Miscellaneous trailer parts
